Simple Overnight Oats

Description

One of my favourite meals to have for breakfast. I rarely start my day without having one of these simple little things. The whole thing takes less than 3 minutes to make, though I advise spending a bit longer to make multiple jars at once to have breakfast sorted for the next few days.

Here are the calories/macros for 1 serving of 456 kcal, 37g protein, 15.2g fat and 39.6g carbs.

Feel free to change the flavour of protein powder if chocolate isn't your thing!

Ingredients

  • 46g porridge oats
  • 120ml whole milk
  • 17g chia seeds
  • 25g chocolate protein powder
  • 92g 0% fat greek yoghurt

Steps

  1. Add 46g of oats to a jar
  2. Add 17g of chia seeds
  3. Shake the jar so the seeds get spread out (it's a pain if you don't do this step)
  4. Add 25g of protein powder
  5. Add 92g of greek yoghurt
  6. Add 120ml of milk
  7. Stir until everything is mixed well
  8. Leave in the fridge overnight

    9. Consume within 3 days just to be on the safe side
