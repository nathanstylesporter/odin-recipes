Simple tuna pesto pasta

Description

Again at the time of starting the Odin Project, I am bulking so this is a very large meal but it is also a very simple one. There is practically no cooking involved yet it is still relatively healthy (can't be doing a dirty bulk) though I can't lie to you and say it's gonna light your taste buds on fire...

I wasn't lying when I said it's a large meal. This provides me with 1 serving of 1,053 kcal, 62.8g protein, 55,4g fat and 73.1g carbs.

Ingredients

  • 150g of spaghetti
  • 2 cans of tuna
  • 190g (the whole can) of red pesto (green is also fine)

Steps

  1. Start cooking the pasta
  2. Once the pasta is cooked, add the pesto and tuna
  3. That's literally it

p.s I don't think you need instructions for cooking pasta. Just add it to boiling water, come back after 10 minutes and just taste it to see if it's done..
