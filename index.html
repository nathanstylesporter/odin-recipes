Beef Chilli Description

This is my go-to meal when I cannot be bothered to cook. The effort to reward ratio is unmatched as all you need to do is add everything into a pan and stir. You can add onions, garlic or coriander but that defeats the point of it being a lazy meal.

I'm on a bulk so this provides me with 4 servings of 744 kcal, 35.5g protein, 25.8g fat and 90.3g carbs.

Total recipe is 2,976 cal, 142g protein, 103g fat, 361.2g carbs so feel free to distribute that however you like!
